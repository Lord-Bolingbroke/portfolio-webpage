Epicodus student handbook

Hi! My name's Noah. I am currently a student at Epicodus, a coding school based in Portland, Oregon. I've created this website to showcase some of the projects I've completed.

I graduated from high school in the Spring of 2018. I was part of a dual-credit program called Summit Learning Charter—in my junior and senior year of high school I was a student at Portland Community College, but the credits I earned there counted towards my high school diploma. While I enjoyed some aspects of PCC, my motivation to study was drained by the inevitable busywork in assignments. I wanted a school program that was more focused, and Epicodus seemed like a good fit.

I had almost no coding experience when I enrolled at Epicodus—just a little HTML and CSS froms school. While I was initially concerned about the the program's fast pace, I was encouraged by the emphasis Epicodus placed on having a growth mindset.

Outside of school/programming, some of my interests include:

  • editing Wikipedia (see my edit history here);
  • exploring Portland by bike;
  • and learning about local history.
